What did we accomplish? Roadblocks? Feedback?

 

During this sprint we focused on fixing bugs that were uncovered after our last sprint as well as ensuring that our code matched the recommended code. In addition, we enabled google analytics in order to track users and gather data to be used in subsequent reports. We also worked to beautify our weekly reports. At this point we are in a good flow and are not encountering and roadblocks or challenges.
