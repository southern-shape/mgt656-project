What did we accomplish? Roadblocks? Feedback?

 

This sprint was incredibly successful because we were able to devote more time to the work this week than we had in previous weeks. In part, this was an effort to get ahead of the curve in anticipation of new user stories that we expect to be added in the coming weeks. We did not experience any major roadblocks.
